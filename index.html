Coolfolio

Project Overview

I created a simple and straightforward portfolio to showcase my skills, knowledge, and projects as a budding developer. This portfolio is a reflection of my dedication to learning and growth, presenting my abilities in a way that is both accessible and visually appealing. It serves as a snapshot of my journey so far, highlighting my determination to master the fundamentals of web development.

The portfolio is designed with clarity and functionality in mind, ensuring a user-friendly experience for visitors. It includes sections that effectively organize my work, making it easy for viewers to explore my projects, skills, and background. By focusing on clean design and intuitive navigation, I aimed to create a platform that not only informs but also engages those who view it.

As a personal milestone, this portfolio symbolizes my transition from a learner to a creator, demonstrating the skills I've acquired and the progress I've made. It also acts as a professional introduction, showcasing my passion for web development and my commitment to continuous improvement. I hope that through this project, visitors can see the potential I bring as a future full-stack developer.

Royale

I developed an online request and order system for Royale Tailoring, allowing customers to book appointments for tailoring services and place orders for product pickups. This website serves as my capstone project for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) program.

Estregan Beach Resort

I developed an online reservation system for Estregan Beach Resort, allowing customers to easily book rooms and cottages. This system streamlines the resort’s reservation process, making it more organized and efficient for the owner. This project is part of my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) coursework.

CKCM Grading System

We designed and implemented the CKCM Grading System, a web-based platform that simplifies the process of managing and calculating student grades. This system enhances efficiency and accuracy for educators, providing an intuitive interface for inputting, updating, and reviewing grades. It was developed as part of my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) coursework during my internship at Christ The King College de Maranding, Inc.
